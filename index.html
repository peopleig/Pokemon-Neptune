Welcome!

  • Use W, A, S, D or the Arrow Keys to move.
  • You'll be provided with 6 starter Pokémons and 2 Potions.
  • The Blue Potion will restore your character's health to 100% mid-battle.
  • The Red Potion will evolve whatever Pokémon you choose by one generation, but not during a battle.
  • You will win some Money after every win
  • Clicking on the Money will open the Shop, clicking it again will close it
  • Battles will occur with random Pokémon if you venture into Dirt/Grass/Sand patches.
  • Winning 5 continuous battles gives you 1 Blue Potion.
  • Winning 10 continuous battles gives you 1 Red Potion.
  • All the Best!
